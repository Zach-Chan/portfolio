Reflection

While working at BlackBerry I was able to exercise and strengthen my skills in teamwork and communication thanks to working in a fast-paced work environment where I closely collaborated with product managers, developers, and other designers. I conducted usability testing, and carried several projects through from start to finish. On other projects I completed the work started by others or began the design process for new features and handed it off to other designers to complete after I left.

Working at BlackBerry also pushed me to keep learning and adding new tools to my arsenal. I became extremely proficient with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator from using them to make red line spec documents and presentations, user-flows, and other means to communicate my decisions to others. I also taught myself Adobe AfterEffects to explore motion design in our mobile interfaces to provide better feedback and moments of delight for users. This helped establish good habits for myself and a mindset to keep exploring and learning new software and tools to complement my design process.
